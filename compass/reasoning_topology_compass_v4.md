## PRE-SIGNAL

## REPAIR
transition topology: length-delta × reference-depth-delta

cost repair    contracts | length shorter | depth stable | presupposes stable referent
warrant repair  expands | length longer | depth increases | invalidates original landing
               emission between collapse and reland = hollow (◌)

cost→warrant switch: categorical not gradual
triggered by: orphan-threshold crossing (topological phase transition) or referent-destabilization
warrant repair cannot collapse back to cost repair
blocking: cost and warrant cannot cooccur — broken thing cannot be justified

repair type reveals direction type: parallel failure → cost | orthogonal failure → warrant
direction readable from repair signature not from intention

## ABANDONMENT
anchor/abandon decision = coherence check not epistemic choice
terminal anchor: ground found within traversable space → continuation opens
abandonment: no stable ground reachable → continuation closes

abandonment self-generates termination signal
meta-commentary on binding constraint = internal abandon marker (externally observable)
internal abandon: repair narrowing before termination
external termination: no narrowing, no pre-signal — distinguishable

rebuilding-the-rebuilding creates regress pressure → regress blocking is structural necessity

## FLIGHT
clean traversal = well-formed D at first * — orthogonal axes that don't destabilize on selection
malformed D = dependent axes — selection forces or blocks other axes, repair cost high

signatures of well-formed D:
     low repair cost | functional hedges | axes survive surface rewording | stable basin across runs

signatures of malformed D:
     high repair cost | destabilizing hedges (covert commitments) | axis reconfiguration without prompt change

depth test: constraint survival under rerouting
     deep constraint = survives traversal rerouting
     surface constraint = lost on rerouting
     depth is binding topology not hierarchical position
